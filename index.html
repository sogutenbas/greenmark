Welcome to GreenMark: Simplifying Your Carbon Emission Calculations

GreenMark is a user-friendly web application designed to help you easily calculate your carbon emissions using straightforward data inputs. By providing simple information about your daily activities, energy consumption, travel habits, and dietary choices, GreenMark offers a clear estimate of your carbon footprint. This term refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases, including carbon dioxide and methane, that are emitted by your actions. It's measured in tonnes of CO2-equivalent (CO2e), symbolizing the environmental impact of individuals, organizations, and products on our planet.
